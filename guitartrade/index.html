Welcome Guitar Traders!

We want to bring an easy way for fellow guitar collectors to trade instruments with one another

What instruments are traded here?: Electric and acoustic guitars and basses. Do we sell the instruments?: No you trade instruments among eachother, so if you see a listing that interests you and you have guitar or bass that you're willing to trade, you can submit your own listing to that persons listing. Can we put in what make or model we are looking for?: Yes you can list what model, year, finish, or specs you are looking for to help filter out listings that you may not be interested in. How does shipping work?: Both parties will pay for their own shipping
